Play Sudoku Online, Free — No Download Required

Sudoku Zen is a free browser-based Sudoku game with seven variants, four board sizes and seven difficulty levels, so it works whether you're picking up a pencil-and-paper classic for the first time or hunting for a genuinely hard puzzle. Every game auto-saves, so you can close the tab mid-solve and pick up exactly where you left off.

How to Play Sudoku

Fill the 9×9 grid so that every row, every column and every 3×3 box contains the digits 1 through 9 exactly once. Some cells start filled in ("givens") and can't be changed; the rest are yours to complete using logic alone — no guessing required. Tap or click a cell, then enter a number with the keypad or your keyboard. Use pencil-mark notes to track candidate numbers in a cell, and lean on hints if you get stuck.

Sudoku variants in Sudoku Zen

  • Classic Sudoku — the standard 9×9 rules.
  • X-Sudoku — adds the two main diagonals as extra regions.
  • Jigsaw Sudoku — irregular jigsaw-shaped regions instead of boxes.
  • Killer Sudoku — cages with target sums replace some of the givens.
  • Hyper Sudoku — four extra shaded 3×3 regions must also hold 1–9.
  • Thermo Sudoku — digits must increase along thermometer paths.
  • Arrow Sudoku — digits along an arrow must sum to the circled cell.

Daily Sudoku Challenge & Leaderboards

Every day, Sudoku Zen generates one puzzle that everyone plays. Finish it to log your time on the global daily leaderboard and see how you stack up against other players — or switch to the weekly and all-time boards to track longer streaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sudoku Zen free to play?

Yes — it's completely free, with no download, no signup and no paywalled puzzles.

Does Sudoku Zen save my progress?

Yes. Your board, statistics, achievements and settings are saved automatically in your browser.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Sudoku Zen is fully responsive and can be added to your home screen as an installable app.
